Project Proposal

synthesize: this is what I understand you want

usually after a meeting about a (new) product

pay close attention to detail in your communication

prevent miscommunication problems with clients

possible issues:

  1. payment
  1. timeline
  1. what they really meant by a feature

it's easier to say no at the beginning than later

don't be afraid to use your technical knowledge to steer the client in a better direction possibly

Polished cover page

  • picture
  • bold title
  • engaging tag line
  • date
  • prepared by (adds detail)
  • prepared for

Introduce key team members

more necessary for the few most important people

  • give client info on individuals that they may be interacting with frequently
    • assume that anything you write may be read by people you'l never interact with
  • team member photos and bios

Table of contents

possibly, if really long project

can make it clickable

choose appropriate sections

Define key terms

can be helpful to writeout

especially for other stakeholders who weren't in meetings

Main sections

Features

main features and how they fit into the web application

possibly how they look and are laid out

Technologies

describe and list the technologies you will use

not really necessary to list git

include logos to make it look pretty :)

can be especially important if any of the technologies have recurring fees

Timeline

nothing should be here that's not in requirements

expectation management

realistic expectations for the client

helpful for setting price
